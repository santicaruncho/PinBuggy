About PinBuggy

PinBuggy is one of Philippine's leading ride-hailing apps that connects more than 1 million users with drivers in more than 100 cities. It currently operates in Ayala Alabang Village.

PinBuggy is part of the mobility joint venture between Mandirigma Marketing, formed in January 2020.
